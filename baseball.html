Baseball and the Weather

In a data engineering course (DE200), we wanted to see if we could find any interesting relationships between weather factors and pertinent outcomes of baseball games. We found a relevant dataset containing game statistics from several thousand MLB games, and scraped weather data from the National Weather Service site that corresponded with the dates of our games of interest. We examined factors like wind and temperature and their possible effects on game outcomes like number of runs and hits by each of the home and visiting teams.
