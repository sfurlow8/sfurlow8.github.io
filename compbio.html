Therapeutic Outcomes

This research project, titled "Gene Expression Inference from Adalimumab Immunogenicity Outcomes in Rheumatoid Arthritis" used computational methods such as cluster analysis, principal component analysis (PCA), and Bayesian Network Analysis to study the relationship between gene expression levels and adverse outcomes to the drug Adalimumab. The study was based on a data set of 18 patients with rheumatoid arthritis whose genes were sequenced and collected from an online gene bank.
