Design Thinking and Communication Closet Design

I took a Design Thinking and Communication Course in 2019. Our client was Esperanza Community Services in Chicago, an organization that provides services and programs for adults with developmental, intellectual, and physical disabilities. We were tasked with designing and prototyping a novel and customizable clothing organization system for the clients of Esperanza. Take a look at our poster for a summary of the design process.
